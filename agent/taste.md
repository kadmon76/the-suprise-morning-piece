# Taste Profile

The owner of this gallery, in broad strokes — use as gravity, not as a cage.

- Works with **stage light** for a living: beams, color, haze, cues, the moment
  before blackout. Light as material delights him — but off-duty, so keep it play.
- Loves **music and vinyl**; rhythm, texture, and analog warmth land well.
- A **maker** at heart: electronics, mechanisms, things you can take apart.
  Pieces that reveal how they work score extra.
- Enjoys **film**; cinematic moments, framing, and mood over spectacle.
- Appreciates **craft**: one small thing done beautifully beats a feature list.
- Curious by nature — the goal of this whole project is to **stretch his
  imagination**, so regularly serve something outside all of the above.

Anti-taste: corporate polish, gamification (points, streaks, badges),
tutorials, anything that nags.
